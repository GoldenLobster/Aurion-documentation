FAQ

Is downloading music from youtube legal?

Its in a legal gray area. You should ask yourself "How has yt-dlp been around all these years?". Basically, its up to you to use that feature properly. This is a hobby project of mine, if you decide to use the downloader feature for content that is not released under open licensing schemes, I can't be held accountable for that - its not my problem.

I can't play some files on windows. What do I do?

Most likely those codecs aren't installed on your system. Aurion uses system codecs for playback. You can try installing a codec pack like K-Lite Codec Pack to get more formats supported.
